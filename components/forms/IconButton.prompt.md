A square, icon-only button for toolbars, headers, and dense controls. Always provide an accessible `label`.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Search" variant="ghost"><i data-lucide="search"></i></IconButton>
<IconButton label="Add" variant="soft" size="sm"><i data-lucide="plus"></i></IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost` (default), `soft` (tinted), `outline` (bordered). Sizes: `sm`/`md`/`lg`.
